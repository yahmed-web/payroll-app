# Brand Identity

## Brand Positioning

Moderator Webnova is an enterprise-grade telecommunications KPI, payroll, commission, analytics, and workforce automation platform designed for modern telecom operations.

The platform focuses on:
- Telecom workforce intelligence
- KPI automation
- Payroll management
- Commission tracking
- Workforce analytics
- Workflow automation
- Excel processing
- Enterprise operational management

The brand represents:
- Intelligence
- Automation
- Precision
- Scalability
- Innovation
- Performance
- Operational excellence

---

# Logo Usage

## Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M" in Moderator on all sides.

---

## Minimum Size

### Digital
- Minimum width: 140px

### Print
- Minimum width: 30mm

---

## Logo Variations

### Primary Logo
- Deep blue logo on light backgrounds

### Secondary Logo
- White logo on dark backgrounds

### Symbol/Icon Version
- Stylized "MW" icon for dashboards, mobile apps, and favicons

---

## Logo Don'ts

- Do not stretch the logo
- Do not rotate the logo
- Do not change typography
- Do not alter approved colors
- Do not apply shadows or gradients
- Do not use low-contrast backgrounds
- Do not compress spacing between letters
